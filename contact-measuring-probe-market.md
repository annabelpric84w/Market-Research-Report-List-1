<p><p>The contact measuring probe market is primarily segmented into machine tool probes and CMM (Coordinate Measuring Machine) probes. Machine tool probes are designed for in-process measurement and are integrated into CNC machines to ensure precise dimensional accuracy during manufacturing. In contrast, CMM probes are used in quality control environments to measure the physical geometrical characteristics of an object with high precision. Both types facilitate increased accuracy and efficiency in various manufacturing and inspection applications, driving advancements in automation and quality assurance.</p></p>
