<p><p>The X-ray diffraction equipment market is categorized into two main types: Powder XRD and Single-crystal XRD. Powder XRD is used for analyzing polycrystalline samples, providing information on phase identification, crystallographic structure, and particle size. It is widely utilized in materials science and chemistry. In contrast, Single-crystal XRD focuses on the structural analysis of individual crystalline samples, offering detailed insights into atomic arrangements and bonding. This technique is essential in fields like solid-state chemistry, mineralogy, and biochemistry.</p></p>
