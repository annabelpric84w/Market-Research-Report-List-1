<p><ul><li>Composite Membranes</li><li>Asymmetric Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semipermeable membrane market is primarily divided into two types: composite membranes and asymmetric membranes. Composite membranes consist of multiple layers that enhance selectivity and permeability, making them ideal for various applications like water purification and gas separation. Asymmetric membranes, on the other hand, feature a non-uniform structure with a dense top layer and a porous support layer, providing efficient separation characteristics. Both types play crucial roles in industrial processes, biomedical applications, and environmental solutions, catering to diverse filtration needs.</p></p>
