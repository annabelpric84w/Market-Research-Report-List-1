<p><p>The explosion-proof camera market is categorized into two main types: integrated explosion-proof cameras and split-type explosion-proof cameras. Integrated explosion-proof cameras combine all components, including the camera and housing, into a single unit, offering ease of installation and maintenance. In contrast, split-type explosion-proof cameras consist of separate components, allowing for more flexible installation in hazardous environments, where space may be limited. Both types ensure safety and reliability in environments with potential explosive gases or dust.</p></p>
