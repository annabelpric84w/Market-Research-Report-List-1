<p><p>The UHT (Ultra-High Temperature) processing machine for dairy products is essential for extending shelf life and ensuring safety. There are two primary types: direct and indirect UHT processing machines. Direct UHT processing involves injecting steam into dairy products, rapidly heating them before cooling. Conversely, indirect UHT processing uses heat exchangers to transfer heat without direct contact with steam, allowing for more controlled temperatures. Both types effectively eliminate harmful microorganisms while preserving the product's nutritional and sensory qualities.</p></p>
