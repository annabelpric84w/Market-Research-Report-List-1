<p><p>The Non-Isolated DC-DC Converter market is categorized into Type I and Type II converters. Type I converters, such as buck and boost converters, allow voltage regulation without electrical isolation, ensuring compact and efficient designs for low-power applications. In contrast, Type II converters, including buck-boost converters, offer versatile voltage output options, accommodating varying input and output combinations. Both types are crucial for industries such as telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics, where space and efficiency are paramount in power management solutions.</p></p>
