<p><p>The copper-based catalysts market is divided into two primary types: powder and lumpy forms. Powder catalysts offer high surface area and reactivity, making them ideal for fine chemical synthesis and processes requiring rapid reactions. Lumpy catalysts, on the other hand, have a larger particle size, which makes them suitable for applications in larger-scale industrial processes, such as petrochemical operations. Both forms play crucial roles in enhancing reaction efficiency and optimizing production across various sectors.</p></p>
