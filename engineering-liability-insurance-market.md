<p><p>Engineering liability insurance encompasses various market types, primarily Directors and Officers (D&O) insurance and Errors and Omissions (E&O) insurance. D&O insurance protects corporate leaders from legal claims arising from their management decisions, safeguarding personal assets against wrongful acts. E&O insurance, on the other hand, covers professional liability for engineers and firms against claims of negligence or inadequate work. Both insurance types are essential for mitigating risks associated with leadership decisions and professional service delivery in the engineering sector.</p></p>
