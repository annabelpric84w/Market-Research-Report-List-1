Powder polyurethane resin provides versatility and uniformity, enabling easy integration into various processes. Flake polyurethane resin, with larger, flat particles, is often utilized for specific binding and coating purposes, enhancing the overall performance of non-woven fabrics in diverse industries.</p></p>
