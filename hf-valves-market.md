<p><strong>The HF Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Check Values</li><li>Gate Values</li><li>Globe Values</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HF valves, crucial in various industrial applications, encompass three main types: check valves, gate valves, and globe valves. Check valves allow fluid flow in one direction, preventing backflow, making them essential for safety. Gate valves provide a straight-flow path, ideal for on/off control in pipelines. Globe valves, with their unique design, enable precise flow regulation, making them suitable for throttling applications. Together, these valves play a vital role in maintaining system efficiency and safety across diverse sectors.</p></p>
