<p><strong>The Common-rail Injection EFI Engine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SDI</li><li>TDI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The common-rail injection EFI engine market primarily features two main types: SDI (Swirl Deposition Injection) and TDI (Turbocharged Direct Injection). SDI focuses on enhancing fuel swirl for better mixing and combustion efficiency, generally used in simpler diesel engines. In contrast, TDI incorporates turbocharging, allowing for higher power output and improved fuel efficiency, making it popular in performance-oriented diesel vehicles. Both technologies aim to optimize engine performance and emissions, catering to specific market demands and regulatory standards.</p></p>
